# Elliott Woods

## Summary

Journalist and author of "Stay Strong, My Brother," a Texas Monthly article published in December 2025 that documented the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster and the stories of survivors, victims, and smugglers involved.

## Details

Elliott Woods is a journalist who wrote the extensive Texas Monthly article "Stay Strong, My Brother" about the San Antonio migrant disaster. His reporting covered the personal stories of survivors like Begaí Santiago Hipólito, the victims including 53 who died, the smugglers and their cartel connections, and the broader context of human smuggling along the US-Mexico border. The article was published in December 2025 and serves as a primary source of information about the disaster.
