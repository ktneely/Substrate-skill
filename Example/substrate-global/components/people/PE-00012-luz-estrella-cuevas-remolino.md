# Luz Estrella Cuevas Remolino

## Summary

Family member of a Mexican migrant who died in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. She represents the families who lost loved ones and had to cope with the aftermath of the smuggling operation.

## Details

Luz Estrella Cuevas Remolino is from Mexico and lost a family member in the San Antonio trailer disaster. She was among the families who dealt with the devastating aftermath — identification of remains, repatriation logistics, and the emotional toll of losing a loved one to the smuggling trade.
