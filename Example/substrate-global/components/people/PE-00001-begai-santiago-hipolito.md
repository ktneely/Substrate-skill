# Begaí Santiago Hipólito

## Summary

Survivor of the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster in which 53 migrants died. Begaí and his brother Mariano traveled from Oaxaca, Mexico, through Guatemala, seeking to reach the United States. He survived the suffocating conditions inside the sealed trailer and was among the handful who lived to tell the story.

## Details

Begaí Santiago Hipólito is from Oaxaca, Mexico. He embarked on the migration journey with his brother Mariano Santiago Hipólito. They paid smugglers (coyotes) to guide them through the border crossing. On June 27, 2022, they were packed into a tractor-trailer in Laredo, Texas, along with over 100 other migrants. The trailer was driven to San Antonio where it was abandoned at a Walmart parking lot on Quintana Road. Begaí survived the lethal conditions — extreme heat, suffocation, lack of water — that killed 53 people including his brother Mariano. His survival testimony provides critical evidence about the smuggling operation and the conditions inside the trailer.
