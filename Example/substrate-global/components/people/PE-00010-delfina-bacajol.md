# Delfina Bacajol

## Summary

Family member of a Guatemalan migrant who died in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. She represents the families left behind who endured the trauma of losing loved ones to the smuggling operation.

## Details

Delfina Bacajol is from Guatemala and lost a family member in the San Antonio trailer disaster. She was among the family members who had to deal with the aftermath — identifying remains, dealing with repatriation, and coping with the loss. Her story illustrates the human cost of the smuggling trade on families in sending communities.
