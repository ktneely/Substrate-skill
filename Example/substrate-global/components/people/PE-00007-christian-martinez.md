# Christian Martinez

## Summary

Recruiter for the Cártel del Noreste smuggling operation who organized migrants for the journey that ended in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. He coordinated the recruitment of migrants and the logistics of the smuggling operation.

## Details

Christian Martinez operated as a recruiter for the Cártel del Noreste smuggling network in the Nuevo Laredo area. He was responsible for identifying and recruiting migrants willing to pay for smuggling services, coordinating their movement through the pipeline, and arranging transportation. He played a key organizational role in the operation that led to the San Antonio disaster. He was prosecuted in connection with the 53 deaths.
