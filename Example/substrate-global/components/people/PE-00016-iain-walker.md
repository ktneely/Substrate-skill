# Iain Walker

## Summary

Lead author of the 2023 research paper coining the term "Motonormativity" — describing how social norms hide dangerous driving behaviour as a major public health hazard.

## Details

Iain Walker is a psychologist who, with co-authors Davis and Tapp, published the seminal paper "Motonormativity: How Social Norms hide a major public health hazard" in the International Journal of Environment and Health (2023). The paper demonstrates how people excuse dangerous driving behaviour due to ingrained social norms, applying double standards to cars versus other activities. The research asked equivalent questions with and without car involvement, revealing systematic bias in how people judge driving behaviour.
