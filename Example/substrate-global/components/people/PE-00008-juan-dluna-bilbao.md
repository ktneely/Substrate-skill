# Juan D'Luna Bilbao

## Summary

Mechanic who worked on the tractor-trailer used in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio migrant disaster. He was prosecuted for his role in preparing the vehicle that was used to transport migrants in lethal conditions.

## Details

Juan D'Luna Bilbao was a mechanic who performed work on the tractor-trailer that was used to smuggle migrants on June 27, 2022. His involvement in preparing the vehicle — which had its ventilation disabled and was sealed to prevent detection — made him a participant in the smuggling operation. He was prosecuted in connection with the 53 migrant deaths.
