# Oslidio López

## Summary

Smuggler (coyote) involved in the migrant smuggling network that operated the pipeline leading to the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster.

## Details

Oslidio López operated as a coyote within the smuggling network that moved migrants from Mexico through the Texas border region. He was part of the organized smuggling operation connected to Cártel del Noreste that treated migrant smuggling as a revenue source. He was involved in the pipeline that resulted in the San Antonio disaster.
