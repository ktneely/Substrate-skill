# Wandsworth Bridge Road Association

## Summary

A community association for Wandsworth Bridge Road (WBR) in SW London, advocating for Clean Green WBR initiatives, active travel, Clean Air Neighbourhoods, and community events including the annual Spring Fayre.

## Details

The WBR Association is a residents' and businesses' association for Wandsworth Bridge Road, a residential high street connecting to a bridge crossing the Thames in SW London. The road has high volumes of through-traffic. The association advocates for urban improvements including parklets, chicanes, Clean Air Neighbourhoods (CAN), and the High Street for All challenge. They publish articles on urban planning topics including motonormativity and the Hill of Hysteria. Contact: info@wbrassociation.org.uk. Active on Facebook and Instagram.
