# PACTS (Parliamentary Advisory Council for Transport Safety)

## Summary

A UK advisory body providing the Manifesto for Road Safety, supporting the ActionVisionZero approach to eliminating road deaths and serious injuries through policy and infrastructure change.

## Details

PACTS is the Parliamentary Advisory Council for Transport Safety, a UK organisation that provides evidence-based advice on transport safety policy. Their Manifesto for Road Safety supports the ActionVisionZero approach and advocates for redesigning urban environments to prioritise people over vehicles. The manifesto provides policy backing for the changes advocated in the WBR article.
