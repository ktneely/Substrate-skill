# Homeland Security Investigations

## Summary

Federal investigative agency under ICE that conducted the criminal investigation into the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. HSI led the effort to identify, arrest, and prosecute those responsible for the 53 migrant deaths.

## Details

Homeland Security Investigations (HSI) is the investigative arm of US Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). Following the San Antonio disaster, HSI led the criminal investigation, working with local law enforcement to identify the smugglers, recruiters, drivers, and others involved in the operation. HSI's investigation resulted in the arrest and prosecution of multiple defendants including Christian Martinez, Felipe Orduña Torres, José Martínez Olvera, Homero Zamorano Jr, and Juan D'Luna Bilbao.
