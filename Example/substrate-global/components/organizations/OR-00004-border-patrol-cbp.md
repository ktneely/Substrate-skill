# Border Patrol (CBP)

## Summary

US federal law enforcement agency under Customs and Border Protection responsible for border security. The agency's "Prevention Through Deterrence" policy has been linked to the lethal conditions that led to the San Antonio trailer disaster.

## Details

US Border Patrol, part of Customs and Border Protection (CBP), is the federal agency responsible for securing the US-Mexico border. Its "Prevention Through Deterrence" strategy, implemented in the 1990s, funneled migration into remote and dangerous terrain, inadvertently increasing reliance on professional smugglers. This policy shift is widely cited as a contributing factor to the rise in lethal smuggling operations like the San Antonio disaster. Border Patrol also operates checkpoints such as Checkpoint 29 near Laredo, which influences smuggling routes and methods.
