# San Antonio Police Department

## Summary

Local law enforcement agency that responded to the discovery of the tractor-trailer containing dead and dying migrants on June 27, 2022. SAPD was among the first responders at the scene on Quintana Road.

## Details

The San Antonio Police Department (SAPD) responded to the discovery of the abandoned tractor-trailer at a Walmart parking lot on Quintana Road on June 27, 2022. Officers and paramedics found 53 dead migrants and dozens more in critical condition inside the sealed trailer. SAPD's response was part of the immediate emergency reaction to what became one of the deadliest single incidents in US human smuggling history.
