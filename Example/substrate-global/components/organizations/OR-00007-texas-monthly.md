# Texas Monthly

## Summary

Texas-based magazine that published "Stay Strong, My Brother" by Elliott Woods in December 2025, a comprehensive journalistic account of the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster and its aftermath.

## Details

Texas Monthly is a prominent magazine covering Texas politics, culture, and news. In December 2025, it published Elliott Woods' article "Stay Strong, My Brother," which provided an in-depth investigation into the San Antonio migrant disaster. The article covered the personal stories of survivors and victims, the smuggling network's operations, the cartel connections, and the broader context of human smuggling along the US-Mexico border. The article serves as the primary source document for this substrate's knowledge about the disaster.
