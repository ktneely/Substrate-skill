# Los Zetas

## Summary

Former Mexican drug cartel that operated in the Nuevo Laredo region. Cártel del Noreste emerged as a splinter group from Los Zetas, inheriting its smuggling routes and operational infrastructure.

## Details

Los Zetas was one of Mexico's most violent drug cartels, originally formed by deserters from the Mexican military's special forces. The cartel controlled significant territory in northeastern Mexico, including the Nuevo Laredo corridor. As Los Zetas fragmented, splinter groups including Cártel del Noreste emerged and took over its smuggling operations. The legacy of Los Zetas' infrastructure and routes continues to shape the human smuggling trade in the region.
