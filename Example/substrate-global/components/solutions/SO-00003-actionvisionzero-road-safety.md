# ActionVisionZero Road Safety

## Summary

A road safety approach aiming to eradicate all deaths and serious injuries from roads by redesigning civic space to be safe for people rather than easy for fast driving, already achieving zero pedestrian deaths in cities like Helsinki and Oslo.

## Details

ActionVisionZero treats road deaths as preventable rather than acceptable. The approach redesigns civic space away from making it easy to drive fast and dangerously toward environments safe for people on bikes and on foot. Helsinki and Oslo have cut pedestrian deaths to zero through this approach. The method involves traffic calming, restricted vehicle access, protected cycleways, and prioritising people over cars in urban design. The approach also delivers co-benefits: improved mental and physical health, well-being, and community connectedness.
