# Cartel Control of Smuggling Routes

## Summary

Cártel del Noreste controls the human smuggling routes through the Nuevo Laredo corridor into Texas, treating smuggling as a revenue-generating enterprise. Cartels tax independent smugglers and run their own operations, creating an organized criminal infrastructure.

## Details

The human smuggling trade along the Texas-Mexico border is controlled by organized criminal cartels, not independent coyotes. Cártel del Noreste dominates the Nuevo Laredo corridor, one of the busiest smuggling routes into the United States. The cartel runs its own smuggling operations and taxes independent operators who want to use its routes. This cartel control transforms smuggling from a small-scale operation into an organized, profit-driven enterprise with the infrastructure to move large numbers of people — but with no regard for their safety.
