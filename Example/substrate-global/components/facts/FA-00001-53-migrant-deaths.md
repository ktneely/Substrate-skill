# 53 Migrant Deaths in San Antonio Trailer

## Summary

53 migrants died in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. The victims included 26 Mexican nationals, 21 Guatemalan nationals, and 6 Honduran nationals. This is the deadliest single incident in US human smuggling history.

## Details

On June 27, 2022, a tractor-trailer containing over 100 migrants was discovered at a Walmart parking lot on Quintana Road in San Antonio, Texas. 53 people were found dead inside the sealed, unventilated trailer. The Bexar County Medical Examiner confirmed the deaths were caused by heatstroke and suffocation. The nationalities of the deceased were: 26 Mexican, 21 Guatemalan, and 6 Honduran. Dozens more survivors were found in critical condition and transported to hospitals.
