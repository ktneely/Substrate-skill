# Migrant Nationalities in San Antonio Disaster

## Summary

The 53 deceased migrants in the San Antonio disaster came from three countries: 26 Mexican nationals, 21 Guatemalan nationals, and 6 Honduran nationals. The diversity of nationalities reflects the broad reach of the smuggling network.

## Details

The victims of the San Antonio trailer disaster came from three Central American and Mexican countries. Mexican nationals (26) represented the largest group, followed by Guatemalan nationals (21), and Honduran nationals (6). This diversity reflects the broad reach of the Cártel del Noreste smuggling network, which recruits migrants from across Central America and southern Mexico. The different nationalities also meant that the aftermath involved coordination with three different governments for identification and repatriation.
