# Criminal Prosecutions

## Summary

Federal criminal prosecutions of those responsible for the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. Multiple defendants including smugglers, recruiters, the driver, and a mechanic were charged and prosecuted for their roles in the deaths of 53 migrants.

## Details

Following the San Antonio disaster, Homeland Security Investigations led a comprehensive criminal investigation. Multiple defendants were prosecuted in federal court: Christian Martinez (recruiter), Felipe Orduña Torres (smuggler), José Martínez Olvera (smuggler), Homero Zamorano Jr (driver), and Juan D'Luna Bilbao (mechanic). The prosecutions demonstrated the organized nature of the smuggling operation and the multiple roles involved — from recruitment to transportation to vehicle preparation. The cases highlighted how human smuggling has become an organized criminal enterprise run by cartels rather than independent coyotes.
