# Checkpoint 29 Influences Smuggling Routes

## Summary

Border Patrol Checkpoint 29, located north of Laredo on Highway 35, is a permanent immigration checkpoint that influences smuggling routes. Smugglers use tractor-trailers and other methods to bypass the checkpoint, contributing to the conditions that led to the San Antonio disaster.

## Details

Checkpoint 29 is a permanent US Border Patrol immigration checkpoint located on US Highway 35 north of Laredo, Texas. All northbound traffic from Laredo passes through this checkpoint. To avoid detection at the checkpoint, smugglers load migrants into sealed vehicles — including tractor-trailers — to transport them past the checkpoint undetected. This practice of sealing migrants inside vehicles directly contributed to the lethal conditions in the San Antonio disaster. The checkpoint's existence creates an incentive for smugglers to use increasingly dangerous transportation methods.
