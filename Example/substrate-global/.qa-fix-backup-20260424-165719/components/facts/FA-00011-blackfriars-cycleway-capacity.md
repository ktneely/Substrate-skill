# Blackfriars Cycleway Capacity Exceeds Road Lanes

## Summary

The protected cycleway on Blackfriars Bridge in London moves nearly 2,000 people per hour in one direction during morning peak within just 2m of width, compared to 1,542 people in the adjacent two traffic lanes — making it 5x more efficient at moving people.

## Details

The Blackfriars Bridge protected cycleway demonstrates the space efficiency of cycling infrastructure versus road lanes for motor vehicles. In the morning peak, the cycleway (2m width) moves nearly 2,000 people per hour in one direction. The adjacent two traffic lanes move 1,542 people. This means the cycleway is 5 times more efficient per unit width at moving people than the road lanes. The data comes from Cycling Embassy analysis of cycle superhighway traffic impacts.
