# Lethal Trailer Conditions

## Summary

The tractor-trailer used in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio disaster was sealed with no ventilation, packed with over 100 migrants in extreme heat exceeding 100°F. The conditions caused death by suffocation and hyperthermia.

## Details

The tractor-trailer that arrived in San Antonio on June 27, 2022, was sealed to prevent detection. The trailer had no ventilation system functioning, no air conditioning, and no water. Over 100 migrants were packed inside in temperatures that exceeded 100°F (38°C) outside, creating even higher temperatures inside the sealed metal container. The combination of heat, lack of oxygen, and overcrowding caused 53 people to die from suffocation and hyperthermia. Survivors reported people screaming, fighting for air, and losing consciousness. The mechanic Juan D'Luna Bilbao had worked on the trailer, including disabling ventilation features.
