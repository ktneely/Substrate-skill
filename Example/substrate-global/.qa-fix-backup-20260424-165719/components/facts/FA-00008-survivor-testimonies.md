# Survivor Testimonies Document Smuggling Operation

## Summary

Survivors of the San Antonio disaster, including Begaí Santiago Hipólito and José Luis Vasquez Guzmán, provided critical testimony about the conditions inside the trailer and the smuggling operation that led to their ordeal.

## Details

The survivors of the San Antonio trailer disaster provided firsthand accounts of the conditions inside the sealed trailer and the smuggling operation. Begaí Santiago Hipólito described the journey from Oaxaca through Guatemala, the payment to smugglers, the loading into the trailer in Laredo, and the horrific conditions inside — extreme heat, suffocation, people dying around him. José Luis Vasquez Guzmán provided corroborating testimony from his experience as a Guatemalan migrant. These testimonies were critical evidence in the criminal prosecutions and in understanding the full scope of the smuggling operation.
