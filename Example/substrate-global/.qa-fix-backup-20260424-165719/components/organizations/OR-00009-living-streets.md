# Living Streets

## Summary

A UK charity advocating for walkable neighbourhoods, producing research including the "Pedestrian Pound" which demonstrates the economic benefits of pedestrian-friendly environments for local businesses.

## Details

Living Streets is a UK charity focused on creating walkable neighbourhoods. Their "Pedestrian Pound" research demonstrates that businesses over-estimate how many customers arrive by car and that pedestrian-friendly environments increase business success. The research provides evidence countering the myth that businesses need car access to flourish. Their work supports the case for pedestrianisation and active travel infrastructure.
