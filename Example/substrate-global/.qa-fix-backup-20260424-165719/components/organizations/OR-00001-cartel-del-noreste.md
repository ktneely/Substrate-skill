# Cártel del Noreste

## Summary

Mexican drug cartel operating in the Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas region that controls significant human smuggling operations across the Texas-Mexico border. The cartel was responsible for the smuggling operation that led to the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster killing 53 migrants.

## Details

Cártel del Noreste (CDN) is a criminal organization based in Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, Mexico. It emerged as a splinter group from Los Zetas and has become one of the dominant cartels in the region. The cartel controls human smuggling routes across the border into Texas, charging migrants thousands of dollars for passage. The smuggling operation is run as a revenue stream, with little regard for migrant safety. CDN was directly responsible for the San Antonio trailer disaster — their recruiters, drivers, and logistics network organized the journey that killed 53 people. The cartel's control of the Nuevo Laredo corridor makes it a key player in the human smuggling trade.
