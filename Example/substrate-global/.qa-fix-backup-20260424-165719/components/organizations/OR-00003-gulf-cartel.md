# Gulf Cartel

## Summary

Mexican drug cartel operating in the border region that competes with Cártel del Noreste for control of smuggling routes. Part of the broader cartel ecosystem that drives lethal human smuggling conditions.

## Details

The Gulf Cartel is one of the oldest and most established Mexican drug cartels, operating primarily in the border region of Tamaulipas. It competes with Cártel del Noreste and other criminal organizations for control of smuggling routes, including human smuggling. The competition between cartels contributes to violence and instability in the border region, making the smuggling trade even more dangerous for migrants.
