# Homero Zamorano Jr

## Summary

The driver of the tractor-trailer in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio migrant disaster. He drove the sealed trailer containing over 100 migrants from Laredo to San Antonio, where he abandoned it at a Walmart parking lot. He was prosecuted for the deaths of 53 migrants.

## Details

Homero Zamorano Jr was hired to drive the tractor-trailer loaded with migrants from Laredo, Texas, to San Antonio. On June 27, 2022, he drove the sealed trailer to a Walmart parking lot on Quintana Road in San Antonio and abandoned it, leaving the migrants trapped inside in extreme heat with no ventilation. When the trailer was discovered, 53 migrants had died from suffocation and hyperthermia. Zamorano was arrested and prosecuted in connection with the deaths.
