# José Martínez Olvera

## Summary

Mexican smuggler (coyote) associated with Cártel del Noreste who was involved in the migrant smuggling operation that led to the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. Prosecuted in connection with the 53 migrant deaths.

## Details

José Martínez Olvera operated as a smuggler in the Nuevo Laredo area, working within the Cártel del Noreste smuggling network. He was involved in organizing and managing the flow of migrants through the smuggling pipeline that resulted in the San Antonio disaster. He faced prosecution for his role in the deaths of 53 migrants.
