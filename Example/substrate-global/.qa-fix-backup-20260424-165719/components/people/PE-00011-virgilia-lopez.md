# Virgilia López

## Summary

Family member of a Mexican migrant who died in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. She endured the trauma of losing a loved one and the difficult process of identification and repatriation.

## Details

Virgilia López is from Mexico and lost a family member in the San Antonio trailer disaster. She was among the Mexican families who had to navigate the aftermath of the disaster — dealing with authorities, identifying remains, and arranging repatriation. Her experience reflects the broader pattern of family devastation caused by the smuggling trade.
