# Mariano Santiago Hipólito

## Summary

One of the 53 migrants who died in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. Brother of survivor Begaí Santiago Hipólito. He traveled from Oaxaca, Mexico, and perished in the sealed trailer due to suffocation and extreme heat.

## Details

Mariano Santiago Hipólito was from Oaxaca, Mexico. He and his brother Begaí embarked on the migration journey together, paying smugglers for passage to the United States. On June 27, 2022, they were packed into a tractor-trailer in Laredo, Texas. The trailer was driven to San Antonio and abandoned. Mariano died from suffocation and hyperthermia in the sealed, unventilated trailer. His body was among the 53 recovered. He was one of 26 Mexican nationals who died in the disaster.
