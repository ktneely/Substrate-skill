# Cristina Ramírez

## Summary

Family member of a migrant who died in the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. She represents the grieving families who lost loved ones to the smuggling operation.

## Details

Cristina Ramírez lost a family member in the San Antonio trailer disaster. She was among the families who had to cope with the devastating loss and navigate the aftermath of the smuggling operation that killed 53 migrants.
