# Felipe Orduña Torres

## Summary

Mexican smuggler (coyote) associated with Cártel del Noreste who was involved in organizing the migrant smuggling operation that led to the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. He was among those prosecuted for the deaths of 53 migrants.

## Details

Felipe Orduña Torres operated as a smuggler in the Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, Mexico area. He was affiliated with Cártel del Noreste, a cartel that controls significant smuggling operations across the Texas-Mexico border. He was involved in recruiting and organizing migrants for the journey that ended in the San Antonio disaster. He was prosecuted in connection with the 53 deaths.
