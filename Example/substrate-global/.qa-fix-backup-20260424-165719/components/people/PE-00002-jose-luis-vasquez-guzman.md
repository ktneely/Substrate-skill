# José Luis Vasquez Guzmán

## Summary

Survivor of the June 27, 2022 San Antonio tractor-trailer disaster. A Guatemalan migrant who was packed into the sealed trailer and survived the lethal conditions that killed 53 people. His testimony provides additional evidence about the smuggling operation and the treatment of migrants.

## Details

José Luis Vasquez Guzmán traveled from Guatemala through Mexico, paying smugglers for passage to the United States. He was loaded into the tractor-trailer in Laredo, Texas, on June 27, 2022. He survived the suffocating, extremely hot conditions inside the sealed trailer when it was abandoned at a San Antonio Walmart parking lot. His account corroborates other survivor testimonies about the conditions and the smuggling network's operations.
