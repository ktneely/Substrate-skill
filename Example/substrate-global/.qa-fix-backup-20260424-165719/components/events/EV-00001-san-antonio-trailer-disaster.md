# San Antonio Tractor-Trailer Disaster

## Summary

On June 27, 2022, a tractor-trailer containing over 100 migrants was abandoned at a Walmart parking lot on Quintana Road in San Antonio, Texas. 53 people died from suffocation and hyperthermia in the sealed, unventilated trailer in extreme heat. It is the deadliest single incident in US human smuggling history.

## Details

On June 27, 2022, a tractor-trailer loaded with migrants in Laredo, Texas, was driven to San Antonio by Homero Zamorano Jr. The trailer was sealed with no ventilation, and the migrants were packed inside in extreme heat (over 100°F). The trailer was abandoned at a Walmart parking lot on Quintana Road. When discovered, first responders found 53 dead bodies and dozens more in critical condition. The victims included 26 Mexican nationals, 21 Guatemalan nationals, and 6 Honduran nationals. Survivors included Begaí Santiago Hipólito and José Luis Vasquez Guzmán. The disaster was organized by Cártel del Noreste smugglers including Christian Martinez, Felipe Orduña Torres, and José Martínez Olvera. The incident led to federal prosecutions and drew international attention to the lethal conditions of human smuggling.
