# Cargo-Bike Last-Mile Delivery

## Summary

Replacing van and HGV deliveries in urban areas with cargo-bike last-mile delivery from out-of-town consolidation hubs, reducing pollution, congestion, road safety risks, and carbon emissions.

## Details

Large delivery vehicles in cities result from lazy logistics — allowing HGVs full access to civic space at all times. Alternative models exist: out-of-town loading areas, last-mile cargo-bike deliveries, and collection hubs. Oxford colleges receive over 100 deliveries per day; they now work with a cargo-bike delivery firm sending all parcels to a central location for once-daily cargo-bike delivery. This achieves massive reductions in van journeys, pollution, congestion, road safety incidents, and carbon emissions. Florists do not need HGV flower delivery; plumbers do not necessarily need vans.
